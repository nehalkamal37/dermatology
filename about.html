About Us

At California Rheumatology Institute , our mission is to provide specialized and comprehensive care for patients with rheumatic conditions. Our goal is to improve the quality of life for our patients through accurate diagnoses, advanced treatments, and personalized care plans tailored to individual needs.

Dr. Solomon Forouzesh, M.D.

“Dedicated to advancing rheumatologic care through compassion and precision.”

Medical Director – California Rheumatology Institute

Member of the Board of Los Angeles Arthritis Foundation

Dr. Forouzesh is an Associate Professor of Medicine and Rheumatology at UCLA/Cedars-Sinai Medical Center and a former Medical Director of the Rehabilitation Department at Southern California Hospital (1998–2016). He has been recognized among Los Angeles’ “Top Doctors” in Rheumatology and was voted the best all-around physician at Southern California Hospital by his peers.

A distinguished Fellow of both the American College of Physicians and the American College of Rheumatology, Dr. Forouzesh brings decades of experience and leadership to the institute.
