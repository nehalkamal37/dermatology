Conditions We Treat

Expert care for a wide range of rheumatic diseases.

Inflammatory Arthritis ▼

Overview: Inflammatory arthritis affects millions worldwide, causing joint pain and stiffness. Our specialists provide innovative treatments, ensuring optimal outcomes.

Symptoms:

  • Joint pain and swelling
  • Morning stiffness
  • Fatigue

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you experience persistent joint pain, swelling, or stiffness affecting daily life, seeking medical evaluation can help prevent long-term complications.

How is It Treated?

  • Advanced medications & biologics
  • Regenerative medicine (PRP, stem cell therapy)
  • Personalized treatment plans

Why Choose Us? Our expert team provides innovative therapies, state-of-the-art diagnostics, and a patient-first approach.

Get Help: Schedule an appointment today to start your journey towards relief.

Ankylosing Spondylitis ▼

Overview: A chronic inflammatory disease affecting the spine, leading to reduced flexibility and discomfort.

Symptoms:

  • Lower back pain
  • Stiffness, especially in the morning
  • Fatigue

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you have prolonged back pain, limited mobility, or stiffness, early intervention can improve outcomes.

How is It Treated?

  • Physical therapy
  • Anti-inflammatory medication
  • Exercise & lifestyle modifications

Why Choose Us? We offer specialized care with the latest treatments for long-term relief.

Get Help: Book a consultation with our specialists.

Psoriatic Arthritis ▼

Overview: A form of arthritis linked to psoriasis, causing joint pain and swelling.

Symptoms:

  • Swollen fingers and toes
  • Joint stiffness
  • Nail changes

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you have joint pain with psoriasis or skin changes, timely treatment can prevent further damage.

How is It Treated?

  • Biologic therapy
  • Targeted medication
  • Lifestyle & dietary changes

Why Choose Us? Our advanced treatments and expert team ensure the best care for psoriatic arthritis.

Get Help: Contact us today to explore treatment options.

Rheumatoid Arthritis ▼

Overview: An autoimmune condition that causes chronic inflammation in joints, leading to pain and stiffness.

Symptoms:

  • Joint tenderness and swelling
  • Morning stiffness lasting more than an hour
  • Fatigue, fever, and weight loss
  • Decreased range of motion

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you experience persistent joint pain, swelling, or stiffness, particularly in multiple joints, it’s important to seek medical attention early to prevent long-term joint damage and improve quality of life.

How is It Treated?

  • Biologic and targeted synthetic disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs (DMARDs)
  • Anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressive therapy
  • Regenerative treatments such as platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy
  • Physical therapy and lifestyle modifications

Why Choose Us?

We provide advanced, patient-centered care using state-of-the-art diagnostics, the latest biologic treatments, and customized therapy plans to help manage RA effectively.

Get Help: Contact us today for a personalized treatment plan that can help you regain mobility and reduce pain.

Systemic Autoimmune Diseases ▼

Overview: Conditions like lupus and Sjögren’s syndrome that affect multiple organs and tissues.

Symptoms:

  • Chronic fatigue
  • Skin rashes and photosensitivity
  • Joint pain and stiffness
  • Dry eyes and dry mouth (specific to Sjögren’s syndrome)
  • Fever and general discomfort

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you experience unexplained fatigue, persistent joint pain, or skin rashes that worsen with sun exposure, seeking medical evaluation early can help manage symptoms effectively and prevent organ damage.

How is It Treated?

  • Immunosuppressive medications and corticosteroids
  • Biologic therapies to regulate immune responses
  • Anti-inflammatory and symptom-specific treatments
  • Supportive therapies like hydration strategies for dry eyes and mouth

Why Choose Us?

Our multidisciplinary team provides cutting-edge treatment options, personalized care, and expert management strategies to improve long-term outcomes for autoimmune conditions.

Get Help: Book a consultation with our specialists today to start managing your autoimmune disease effectively.

Metabolic and Crystal-Induced Diseases ▼

Overview: A metabolic disorder causing sudden and severe joint pain due to uric acid buildup.

Symptoms:

  • Severe pain in the big toe or other joints
  • Swelling, redness, and warmth in the affected area
  • Sudden onset of intense discomfort, usually at night

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you experience repeated gout attacks, persistent joint pain, or notice swelling and redness in your joints, seek medical attention to prevent complications such as joint damage.

How is It Treated?

  • Medications to reduce uric acid levels and prevent flare-ups
  • Anti-inflammatory drugs to relieve acute pain and swelling
  • Lifestyle modifications, including dietary adjustments to reduce purine intake
  • Hydration therapy to aid uric acid excretion

Why Choose Us?

Our clinic offers advanced diagnostic tools and personalized treatment plans to effectively manage gout and prevent future flare-ups.

Get Help: Schedule an appointment today to receive expert guidance on gout management and treatment.

Systemic Lupus Erythematosus ▼

Overview: Lupus is a systemic autoimmune disease where the immune system attacks healthy tissues, affecting the skin, joints, and organs.

Symptoms:

  • Fatigue and joint pain
  • Butterfly-shaped facial rash
  • Kidney, heart, and lung complications

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you have persistent joint pain, skin rashes, or unexplained fatigue, consult a specialist for early diagnosis.

How is It Treated?

  • Immunosuppressive medications
  • Anti-inflammatory treatments
  • Lifestyle adjustments to manage symptoms

Why Choose Us? Our lupus specialists provide expert diagnostics and advanced treatment plans to manage symptoms effectively.

Get Help: Book a consultation for lupus management.

Osteoarthritis ▼

Overview: Osteoarthritis is a degenerative joint disease that causes cartilage breakdown, leading to joint pain and stiffness.

Symptoms:

  • Joint pain and stiffness
  • Swelling and decreased range of motion
  • Cracking sounds in joints

When Should I See a Doctor?

If you experience joint stiffness, pain during movement, or limited mobility, seek medical attention for pain management options.

How is It Treated?

  • Pain relief medications and injections
  • Physical therapy to maintain joint function
  • Weight management and lifestyle modifications

Why Choose Us? Our clinic offers advanced non-invasive treatments, including regenerative medicine and physical therapy.

Get Help: Contact us today to explore osteoarthritis treatment options.
